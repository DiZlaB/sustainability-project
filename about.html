About me

Hi, I’m Dilyana — the creator of LOFLEEF.

This project grew from a simple belief: that every small action we take leaves a mark, and we should choose those marks with care. During my journey of learning web development and exploring sustainability, I realized how deeply connected we all are to water, soil, plastic, and the technologies shaping our future. LOFLEEF is my way of understanding these topics, sharing what I learn, and building something meaningful along the way. LOFLEEF is not a company — it’s a personal project, a learning space, and a small reminder that we are constantly shaping the world around us. If we leave a trail, may it be one that supports life, not harms it.
Thank you for visiting, reading, and supporting this little corner of the web.
